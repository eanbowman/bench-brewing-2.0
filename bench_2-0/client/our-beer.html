OUR BEERS

The beer from Ontario’s wine country. Bench Brewing beer is a unique combination of the farmhouse approach to craft brewing and the local winemaking tradition of the Niagara region. In Ontario’s fruit land, the fertile ecosystem of the land will impart a unique “terroir” for our beer, just as it does for wine.

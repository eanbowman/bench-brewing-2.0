STONE ROAD WHITE ALE

"Located along the Benchlands of the Niagara Escarpment, we at Bench Brewing are passionate about the beer we make and the place we make it. Our White Ale is named after the old Queenston to Grimsby Stone Road that originally brought Loyalist and Mennonite settlers to the Twenty Valley. Today, this historical path is part the Niagara Wine Route known as King Street or Regional Road 81. It is also where we call home. With a hazy, white appearance, our Belgian-style wheat beer has aromas of orange peel and spice, and a complex yet balanced finish."

4.5% abv


IBU 30


Yeast: Vermont Ale


Kettle Hops: Simcoe & Mosaic


Dry Hops: Simcoe & Mosaic

Malts: Golden Promise
Carafoam
Wheat



Suggested Food Pairings: Pizza Margherita
Buffalo Wings
Steak Salad
